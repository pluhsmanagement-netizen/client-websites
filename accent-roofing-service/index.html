FAQ

Common questions answered.

What areas do you serve?
Accent Roofing Service is based in Lawrenceville, GA and proudly serves communities throughout Gwinnett County and the greater Atlanta metro area — including Buford, Snellville, Duluth, Suwanee, Grayson, Loganville, and Athens. Call us to confirm coverage in your neighborhood.

How do I know if my roof needs to be repaired or replaced?
Common signs include missing or curling shingles, interior leaks, granules in your gutters, or a roof over 20 years old. Our team provides free, no-pressure inspections to give you an honest assessment of whether a repair or full replacement is the right solution for your home.

Do you offer financing for roofing projects?
Yes! Accent Roofing Service offers flexible financing options to help make your roofing project affordable. We work with a variety of financing programs to fit different budgets — ask us about your options when you schedule your free estimate.

What happens if there's a roof repair emergency?
We offer 24/7 live answering — so when a storm hits or a leak appears, a real person is always available to help. Our team will triage your situation, provide emergency tarping if needed, and schedule prompt repairs to prevent further damage to your home.

Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Accent Roofing Service is fully licensed and insured in the state of Georgia. Our team is also GAF Master Elite certified and HAAG certified for storm damage inspections, giving our customers complete peace of mind throughout every project.

Can you help me navigate my insurance claim?
Yes — our specialists are experienced in working with insurance adjusters and can help document storm damage, prepare estimates, and advocate on your behalf to ensure a fair settlement. We've helped thousands of Georgia homeowners navigate this process with confidence.
