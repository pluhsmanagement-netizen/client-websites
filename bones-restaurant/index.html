— GOT QUESTIONS? —

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

DO YOU ACCEPT WALK-INS OR IS RESERVATION REQUIRED?
We welcome both walk-ins and reservations, though we strongly recommend booking ahead during peak hours and weekends to guarantee your preferred table. Reservations can be made by calling us directly at 07131 873405. Walk-in availability depends on capacity at the time of your visit.
WHAT ARE YOUR OPENING HOURS?
We are open Monday through Thursday from 12:00–2:30 PM and 5:00–11:00 PM. Friday hours are 5:00–11:30 PM, Saturday 12:00–11:30 PM, and Sunday 5:00–10:00 PM. We are open seven days a week so there is always a perfect time to visit.
DO YOU CATER FOR SPECIAL OCCASIONS AND EVENTS?
Absolutely — we specialize in making celebrations unforgettable. Whether it is a birthday, anniversary, or corporate event, our team will coordinate table decorations, personalized menus, and dedicated service. Contact us in advance so we can prepare everything to your exact specifications.
WHAT TYPES OF STEAKS DO YOU SERVE?
Our menu celebrates premium cuts including Ribeye, dry-aged Flank Steak, Rinderfleisch, and seasonal specialty cuts. All steaks are prepared by our expert kitchen team and cooked precisely to your requested doneness. We also offer accompaniments like herb butter, salads, and premium sides.
DO YOU OFFER VEGETARIAN OR NON-MEAT OPTIONS?
While we are a steak-focused restaurant, we do offer a selection of hearty salads, premium sides, and non-beef protein options to accommodate guests with different preferences. Please call ahead or ask your server for the current available alternatives. We are always happy to accommodate dietary requirements.
IS PARKING AVAILABLE NEAR THE RESTAURANT?
Yes, there are parking options available in the surrounding area near Sülmerstraße 40. We recommend arriving a few minutes early to allow time to find a convenient spot. Public transportation connections are also readily accessible for those coming from across the Atlanta area.
