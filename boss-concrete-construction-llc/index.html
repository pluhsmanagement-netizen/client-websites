FREQUENTLY ASKED

HOW LONG DOES A CONCRETE DRIVEWAY INSTALLATION TAKE?

A standard residential driveway typically takes 1-2 days to pour and form, plus an additional 24-48 hours before light foot traffic and up to 7 days before vehicle use. We'll give you a specific timeline during your free quote based on your project size and current weather conditions in the Tyrone, GA area.

WHAT THICKNESS SHOULD MY CONCRETE SLAB BE?

For residential driveways, we typically pour at 4 inches thick with proper rebar reinforcement. Slabs for heavy vehicles or equipment are poured at 6 inches or more. We assess each project individually and always recommend the right thickness for your specific load and use requirements — no cutting corners on our end.

DO YOU OFFER FREE ESTIMATES?

Absolutely — every estimate we provide is 100% free with no obligation. We'll come out to your property in Tyrone, GA or the surrounding area, assess the scope of work, and give you a transparent, written quote on the spot. We believe in upfront pricing with zero surprises.

ARE YOU LICENSED AND INSURED IN GEORGIA?

Yes. BOSS Concrete Construction LLC is fully licensed, insured, and bonded to operate in Georgia. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance to protect your property and our crew on every project. You can request proof of insurance before we begin — we're happy to provide it.

CAN CONCRETE BE POURED IN COLD OR HOT WEATHER?

Concrete can be poured in a wide range of temperatures, but extreme heat or cold requires special precautions. In Georgia's climate, we monitor weather closely and adjust our mix and curing process accordingly. We'll never pour in conditions that would compromise the quality of your finished product — your project's integrity always comes first.

HOW DO I MAINTAIN MY NEW CONCRETE DRIVEWAY OR PATIO?

Proper maintenance starts with sealing your concrete every 2-3 years to protect it from moisture, stains, and Georgia's UV exposure. Avoid harsh deicers and clean up oil spills promptly. We offer professional sealing and coating services, and we'll walk you through a care plan after every project is complete so your investment lasts for decades.
