How do I know if a tree on my Atlanta property needs to be removed?

Signs that a tree may need removal include large dead branches, significant lean toward structures, visible trunk decay, fungal growth at the base, or roots lifting pavement. A certified arborist assessment is always the best first step — many trees that appear declining can be saved with proper care. Our Boutte Tree arborists will give you an honest evaluation and only recommend removal when truly necessary.

Does Boutte Tree provide emergency service after storms?

Yes — Boutte Tree operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays, for emergency storm response throughout Atlanta and the surrounding metro area. If a tree or large limb has fallen on your home, vehicle, or is blocking access, call us immediately at (404) 799-5472. Our crews mobilize quickly to secure your property and remove hazards safely.

What is the best time of year to prune trees in Georgia?

For most deciduous trees in Georgia, late winter — when trees are dormant — is the ideal pruning window as it minimizes stress and promotes vigorous spring growth. However, dead, diseased, or hazardous limbs can and should be removed any time of year. Flowering trees like dogwoods and cherries are best pruned immediately after they bloom. Our arborists will advise you on species-specific timing during your consultation.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Atlanta, GA?

Absolutely. Boutte Tree, Inc. is fully licensed in the state of Georgia and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage, protecting both your property and our crew. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request before any work begins. We strongly encourage all homeowners to verify insurance with any tree service company they consider hiring.

How much does tree removal or trimming cost in Atlanta?

Tree service pricing in Atlanta varies based on tree size, species, location, proximity to structures, and access to your property. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for every job — what you're quoted is what you pay, with no hidden fees. Our pricing is competitive with the Atlanta market, and we believe in transparent, fair quotes that reflect the level of expertise and care our certified arborists bring to every project.

Boutte Tree serves Atlanta and the surrounding metro area including Decatur, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Smyrna, Tucker, Chamblee, Norcross, East Point, College Park, and beyond. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, give us a call at (404) 799-5472 — we're happy to confirm service availability and get your free estimate scheduled as soon as possible.
