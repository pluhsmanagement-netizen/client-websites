Meet the Bouttes.

A family-owned business led by an ISA Board Certified Master Arborist and his wife — running Boutte Tree together since 2005.

ISA Master Arborist SO-5119B
Founder & Co-Owner

Tierson Boutte

Tierson, founder and co-owner of Boutte Tree, Inc., was born in Manhattan, NY and grew up in Washington, DC until the age of twelve, when his family moved to upstate New York. He finished high school in a small mountain town called Keene Valley — a popular ski destination, and the place where he was first trained in (and finally mastered) snow shoveling.

After his freshman year at Georgia Tech studying engineering and a disastrous internship in a light switch manufacturing facility, he went home for the summer to work in the view-pruning business for Sawteeth, a local company owned by Dan Moore. He learned the ropes there, and when he returned to school he took a job with Smith's Tree Service in Austell, GA, where he was introduced to the removal side of the business. After a while, Tierson left and began offering his own services to Atlanta residents.

Boutte Tree came into existence shortly thereafter. At first very small, Tierson watched the company grow little by little. After he graduated in 2002 with a degree in civil engineering, he devoted all of his energies to the business — and shortly afterward became certified as an arborist.

Today, Tierson handles the bulk of safety training and employee development for the field staff while still keeping a hand in daily production. He's involved in regional training efforts and collaborates with the Georgia Arborist Association, the Crane Safety Institute, and other local organizations to improve safety and skill levels across the industry. His hobbies include wood splitting and rope splicing.

Co-Owner

Jessa Boutte

In 2005, Tierson married Jessa, and they now own and manage Boutte Tree together. The Bouttes have two daughters who are both avid tree climbers — the whole family loves trees.
