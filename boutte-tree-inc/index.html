Frequently Asked

Questions worth answering.

Do I need a permit to remove a tree in Atlanta?

Atlanta has a Tree Protection Ordinance that may require permits for removing trees above a certain diameter, especially on properties zoned residential. Our arborists are familiar with city regulations and can advise you on what's required before any work begins. We handle the paperwork when needed.

How quickly can you respond to a fallen or hazardous tree?

We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency situations. For fallen trees, active roof damage, or blockages on access routes, we typically dispatch within the hour depending on crew availability. Call (404) 799-5472 directly for the fastest response.

What's the difference between a tree trimmer and a certified arborist?

ISA Certified Arborists have passed a rigorous examination covering tree biology, diagnosis, pruning standards, and risk assessment. A general tree trimmer may have practical experience but lacks the formal training to identify disease, structural failure, or hazard risk. Boutte Tree employs certified arborists who approach every project with that deeper level of knowledge.

Is Boutte Tree fully insured?

Yes. We carry full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We're happy to provide certificates of insurance before any project begins — many property managers and HOAs require this, and we keep documentation readily available for your peace of mind.

Can you save a tree that looks sick or is leaning dangerously?

Often, yes. Many trees that appear diseased or structurally compromised can be treated with proper pruning, cabling, fertilization, or soil remediation. Our arborists assess each situation thoroughly before recommending removal — we only remove trees when it's truly the safest or only viable option.

Do you clean up after the job?

Absolutely. Cleanup is included in every service. Our crews rake, blow, and haul all debris from your property — branches, chips, and logs. We leave your yard in better shape than we found it. Several of our reviews specifically mention our cleanup thoroughness, and we take that seriously.
