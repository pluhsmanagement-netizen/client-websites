Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical fence installation take in Marietta?
Most residential fence installations are completed within one to two days depending on the linear footage and material type. Larger commercial projects or custom designs may take longer, and our team will provide a clear timeline during the estimate process so you can plan accordingly.
Do I need a permit to install a fence in Marietta, GA?
Permit requirements vary by fence height and property type within Cobb County and the City of Marietta. Our team is familiar with local regulations and will advise you on what is required before any work begins. We can assist with the permit process to keep your project on schedule.
What fence material is best for Georgia's climate?
Georgia's heat, humidity, and occasional storms put stress on all fencing materials. Vinyl and aluminum are extremely low-maintenance in this climate, while pressure-treated wood and cedar hold up well when properly installed and sealed. We'll help you choose the right material based on your budget, aesthetics, and how much upkeep you want to manage.
Can Bravo Fence handle large commercial or multi-property projects?
Absolutely. We work with property managers, developers, and business owners across Metro Atlanta on large-scale commercial fencing projects. Our crews are equipped to handle significant linear footage with coordinated scheduling to minimize disruption to daily operations.
How do I get a quote from Bravo Fence Company?
Simply call us at (770) 966-9970 or submit the form on our contact page and a representative will reach out to schedule your free in-person estimate. Most consultations are available within a few business days and there is never any obligation to move forward after receiving your quote.
What happens if my fence is damaged after installation?
We stand behind every fence we install. If you experience any issues with workmanship or materials, contact us directly and we will schedule a time to assess and correct the problem. Customer satisfaction is our top priority and we have a track record of resolving concerns quickly and professionally.
