FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?
Cool Air Mechanical serves the greater Marietta area including East Cobb, Kennesaw, Smyrna, Vinings, Powder Springs, Mableton, Sandy Springs, Roswell, Woodstock, and Acworth. If you're in the greater Atlanta metro and aren't sure if you're in our service zone, give us a call at (470) 531-5480 and we'll confirm right away.

Do you charge extra for after-hours or emergency HVAC service?
No — Cool Air Mechanical has a firm policy of no after-hour fees on HVAC services, ever, for residential customers. Whether you call at 2am on a Sunday or during a holiday, you'll be charged standard rates. This is one of the key ways we set ourselves apart from other Marietta HVAC companies.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance in Georgia?
We recommend at least two tune-ups per year — once in spring before cooling season and once in fall before heating season. Georgia's hot, humid summers put extra strain on AC systems, so regular maintenance is especially important for homeowners in the Marietta area to prevent breakdowns and maintain efficiency.

How long does a full HVAC system replacement take?
Most residential HVAC replacements are completed within one day. Our installation crews are experienced, efficient, and come fully equipped with the right tools and replacement equipment. In some cases involving ductwork modifications or complex systems, the job may extend to two days.

Does Cool Air Mechanical offer financing for new HVAC systems?
Yes, Cool Air Mechanical partners with Costco and other financing programs to help Marietta homeowners afford quality HVAC systems. Our comfort consultants will walk you through all available options, including any current promotions or financing rates, during your free in-home assessment.

Why choose Cool Air Mechanical over other Marietta HVAC companies?
Cool Air Mechanical has served the Marietta community since 2016, earning over 10,000 five-star Google reviews. We offer 24/7 availability with zero after-hour fees, licensed and insured technicians, and a full suite of home comfort services — HVAC, plumbing, and electrical — so you only need one trusted company for your home.
