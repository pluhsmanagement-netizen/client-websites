— Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of construction projects does Cunningham Associates handle?

Cunningham Associates handles a wide range of construction work, including general contracting, new construction, commercial build-outs, and structural work. We also offer full remodeling services — kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and whole-home transformations — along with specialty projects like custom carpentry, design-build, historic restoration, and tenant improvements.

Are you available for emergency or after-hours construction needs?

Yes — Cunningham Associates is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week, including weekends and holidays. Whether you're facing an urgent structural issue or have a project that requires after-hours attention, our team is always available to take your call and respond promptly.

What areas around Atlanta do you serve?

We serve Atlanta and the greater metro area, including Decatur, Marietta, Smyrna, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Tucker, Roswell, Norcross, and Chamblee. If you're within 30 miles of Atlanta, GA, we can very likely assist you — please call us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Do you provide free estimates or consultations?

Yes, we offer initial consultations to assess your project's scope and discuss your goals and budget. Our team will provide an honest, transparent quote before any work begins so there are never any surprises. Simply call us at (770) 343-7565 or submit the contact form on this page to get started.

Is Cunningham Associates licensed and insured in Georgia?

Yes. Cunningham Associates operates as a licensed general contractor in the state of Georgia and carries full insurance coverage. Our clients can proceed with confidence knowing that every project is backed by the appropriate credentials, liability coverage, and professional accountability that quality construction demands.

How do you approach communication during a project?

We believe consistent communication is the foundation of every successful project. From the initial consultation through project completion and follow-up, we keep you informed at every milestone. Our clients regularly note in reviews that our team is prompt, clear, and genuinely attentive — we consider that the standard, not the exception.
