What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing? +
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to clean hard, durable surfaces like driveways, sidewalks, and patios. Soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with safe cleaning solutions to treat delicate surfaces such as roofs, siding, and windows. We'll recommend the right method for your specific surface to ensure the best results without any damage.
