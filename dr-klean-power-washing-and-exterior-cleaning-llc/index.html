FAQ

Common Questions.

What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast away stubborn dirt, oil, and stains from hard surfaces like concrete and brick. Soft washing uses low pressure combined with eco-friendly cleaning solutions to safely clean delicate surfaces like roofs, siding, and painted wood without causing damage.
How often should I have my home pressure washed in Atlanta?
In Atlanta's humid climate, most homes benefit from exterior cleaning once a year to keep mold, mildew, and pollen buildup under control. High-traffic areas like driveways and patios may benefit from cleaning twice a year depending on usage and tree cover.
Is pressure washing safe for my landscaping and plants?
Yes — we take great care to protect your landscaping during every job. We pre-wet plants and shrubs before applying any cleaning solutions and rinse thoroughly afterward. Our biodegradable products are formulated to be safe for vegetation, pets, and people.
Do you offer free estimates?
Absolutely — we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial projects. Simply give us a call at (678) 954-9367 or fill out our online contact form and we'll get back to you promptly with transparent, upfront pricing.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, Dr. Klean Power Washing and Exterior Cleaning LLC. is fully licensed and insured to operate in Atlanta, GA. Our coverage protects your property and gives you peace of mind knowing you're working with a professional, accountable service provider.
How long does a typical job take?
Most residential jobs — like driveway cleaning or house washing — take between one to three hours depending on the size and condition of the surface. Larger or more complex projects such as full-home exterior cleaning including roof, deck, and walkways may take a half day or more.
