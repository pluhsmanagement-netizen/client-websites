Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How often should I have my home pressure washed in Atlanta?
Most Atlanta homes benefit from a professional pressure washing once a year due to the city's humid climate, which promotes rapid algae, mold, and pollen buildup. Homes surrounded by trees or in shaded areas may need cleaning twice per year to stay on top of organic growth.
What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to remove stubborn dirt and stains from hard surfaces like concrete, brick, and driveways. Soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with specialized cleaning solutions to safely treat delicate surfaces such as roofs, siding, and painted wood without causing damage.
Is pressure washing safe for my roof and shingles?
Traditional high-pressure washing is not recommended for asphalt shingles as it can strip protective granules and void manufacturer warranties. We use a gentle soft wash method on all roofs, which effectively kills moss, algae, and lichen at the root level without any risk of shingle damage.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial jobs in the Atlanta area. Simply call us at (678) 954-9367 or fill out our contact form and we'll get back to you promptly, often the same day.
How long does a typical pressure washing job take?
Job duration varies based on the size and scope of the project. A standard driveway or patio cleaning typically takes 1–2 hours, while a full house wash with roof and deck can take 3–5 hours. We always prioritize thoroughness over speed to deliver the best possible results.
Are your cleaning products safe for my plants and pets?
We use biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for landscaping, pets, and the environment when applied correctly. As a standard precaution, we recommend keeping pets indoors and away from the work area during the cleaning process and until surfaces have fully dried.
