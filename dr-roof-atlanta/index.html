Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a full roof replacement or just a repair?

The answer depends on the age of your roof, the extent of damage, and how many layers are already present. Roofs with widespread granule loss, multiple leaks, or more than 20 years of service typically require replacement, while isolated damage to a small area may be repairable. Dr Roof Atlanta provides honest, comprehensive inspections to help you make the right decision — not the most expensive one.

How long does a roof replacement take in Roswell, GA?

Most standard residential roof replacements in the Roswell area are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. Larger homes, multiple roof pitches, or the presence of skylights and chimneys may extend the timeline. We communicate a clear schedule before work begins so you know exactly what to expect.

Can Dr Roof Atlanta help with my insurance claim after storm damage?

Yes — our team is experienced with the insurance claim process and can walk you through each step from initial documentation through adjuster coordination and final sign-off. We provide thorough written damage reports and work directly with your insurance company to ensure nothing is overlooked. Many of our customers have found we catch damage their adjuster initially missed.

What roofing materials do you recommend for homes in Georgia?

Architectural asphalt shingles are the most popular choice in Georgia for their balance of durability, aesthetics, and value. For homeowners looking for longer-term performance, standing seam metal roofing offers exceptional longevity and energy efficiency suited for the region's heat and humidity. We'll discuss your home's specific needs, budget, and style preferences during your free estimate.

Do you offer emergency roofing services in Roswell?

Absolutely. Dr Roof Atlanta is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency situations including storm damage, fallen trees, and active leaks threatening interior spaces. We can deploy emergency tarping quickly to protect your home while permanent repairs are scheduled. Call (770) 450-8843 any time for immediate assistance.

How much does a new roof cost in the Roswell, GA area?

Roofing costs vary based on square footage, pitch, material choice, and the condition of existing decking. We provide free, itemized estimates with transparent pricing so you understand exactly what you're paying for before any work begins. Our goal is to offer competitive, fair pricing — and our 1,787 reviews reflect that we consistently deliver value homeowners are satisfied with.
