GOT QUESTIONS?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

HOW DO I KNOW IF MY ROOF NEEDS REPLACEMENT OR JUST REPAIRS? + −
If your roof is over 20 years old, has widespread shingle damage, or has experienced repeated leaks in multiple areas, full replacement is usually more cost-effective than ongoing repairs. Our free inspection will give you an honest, detailed assessment so you can make an informed decision — never a sales pitch.
DO YOU WORK WITH INSURANCE COMPANIES FOR STORM DAMAGE CLAIMS? + −
Yes — we have extensive experience navigating homeowner insurance claims for storm, hail, and wind damage. Our team documents damage thoroughly, communicates directly with adjusters, and guides you step-by-step from initial filing to final payment. You don't have to fight the insurance company alone.
HOW LONG DOES A FULL ROOF REPLACEMENT TAKE? + −
Most residential roof replacements in the Roswell area are completed in one to two days depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We schedule efficiently, arrive on time, and work without unnecessary delays — your home won't be exposed longer than it has to be.
ARE YOU LICENSED AND INSURED IN GEORGIA? + −
Absolutely. Dr Roof Atlanta is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Georgia. We carry both general liability and workers' compensation coverage, so you're completely protected if anything unexpected occurs on your property during the job.
WHAT ROOFING MATERIALS DO YOU INSTALL? + −
We install asphalt shingles, metal roofing systems, and flat/low-slope materials suited to both residential and commercial properties in Roswell and the Atlanta metro area. During your free estimate, we'll recommend the best material for your home's needs, budget, and HOA requirements.
DO YOU OFFER EMERGENCY ROOFING SERVICES? + −
Yes — we're available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency roofing situations. Whether it's a fallen tree, storm breach, or sudden leak, we respond fast with emergency tarping and temporary repairs to protect your home until permanent work can be scheduled.
