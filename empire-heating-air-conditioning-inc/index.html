Common Questions

FAQ

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

Most HVAC professionals recommend scheduling maintenance at least twice a year — once in spring for your cooling system and once in fall for your heating system. Regular tune-ups extend equipment life, improve efficiency, and help catch small issues before they become expensive repairs.

How do I know if I need to repair or replace my AC unit?

As a general rule, if your unit is over 10–15 years old and the repair cost exceeds half the price of a new system, replacement is usually the smarter investment. Our technicians will always give you an honest assessment with pricing for both options so you can make the best decision for your budget.

We are based in Decatur, GA and serve the greater Atlanta metro area including Tucker, Clarkston, Stone Mountain, Avondale Estates, Lithonia, Chamblee, Doraville, Dunwoody, and surrounding communities. Call us at (404) 294-0900 to confirm service in your area.

Do you offer same-day HVAC service?

We do our best to accommodate same-day appointments when our schedule allows. Many customers have been able to get service the same afternoon they called. Contact us early in the day for the best chance of same-day availability at (404) 294-0900.

What should I do if my AC stops working on a hot day?

First, check that your thermostat is set correctly and your circuit breaker hasn't tripped. Replace your air filter if it's dirty, and ensure all vents are open. If the issue persists, call Empire at (404) 294-0900 and we'll get a technician out to diagnose and fix the problem as quickly as possible.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes. All Empire Heating & Air Conditioning technicians are fully licensed and the company carries comprehensive insurance. You can trust that every job is performed to code, with your safety and satisfaction as the top priority.
