Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates for fence installation?
Yes, Fox Fence Company provides free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial fencing projects in Acworth, GA and the surrounding area. A representative will visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed quote tailored to your specific situation.
What types of fencing do you install?
We install a wide range of fence types including wood privacy fences, vinyl fencing, chain link, aluminum, ornamental iron, split rail, and custom decorative designs. Whether you need a backyard privacy fence or a commercial security perimeter, we have the expertise to handle it.
How long does a typical fence installation take?
Most residential fence installations are completed within one to two days, depending on the size of the project and material type. Larger commercial projects or complex custom designs may take longer, and we'll always give you a clear timeline before work begins.
Do I need a permit to install a fence in Acworth, GA?
Permit requirements vary depending on your specific location, fence height, and property type. We are familiar with local regulations in Acworth and surrounding Cherokee and Cobb County areas, and we can help guide you through the permitting process if required for your project.
Can you work with my HOA's requirements?
Absolutely. Our team is experienced in coordinating with HOA boards and meeting community fence guidelines. We assist with documentation, material selections, and designs that align with your HOA's specific requirements so the approval process goes smoothly.
Do you repair existing fences, or only install new ones?
We handle both repairs and full installations. From replacing a few rotted boards to fixing storm-damaged sections, post replacements, and gate repairs — our team can restore your existing fence efficiently and at a fair price, often without needing a full replacement.
