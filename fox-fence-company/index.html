FAQ

HOW MUCH DOES FENCE INSTALLATION COST IN ACWORTH?
Fence installation costs in Acworth vary depending on the material, fence length, and terrain. Wood privacy fencing typically ranges from $20–$45 per linear foot installed, while vinyl and aluminum options can range higher. We always provide a free, detailed estimate before any work begins so you know exactly what to expect.
DO I NEED A PERMIT TO BUILD A FENCE IN ACWORTH, GA?
In many cases, a permit is required for fence installation in Acworth and Cobb County depending on height and location. Our team is familiar with local permitting requirements and can advise you on what's needed for your specific project. We handle the process to keep your installation on track and code-compliant.
HOW LONG DOES FENCE INSTALLATION TAKE?
Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days depending on the size of the property and the type of fence. Our experienced crews work efficiently without cutting corners, and we always confirm the estimated timeline with you during the free estimate process.
WHAT TYPES OF FENCES DO YOU INSTALL?
Fox Fence Company installs wood, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, ornamental iron, and custom decorative fencing. We also specialize in pool fencing and commercial fencing projects of all sizes. Whatever your need, our team has the experience and materials to get the job done right.
DO YOU OFFER FREE ESTIMATES?
Yes — absolutely. We provide free, no-obligation on-site estimates for all fence installation and repair projects in the Acworth area. One of our knowledgeable representatives will visit your property, assess the job, and walk you through your options before providing a written quote.
IS FOX FENCE COMPANY LICENSED AND INSURED?
Yes. Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in Georgia. We carry general liability insurance and comply with all state and local regulations. You can hire us with confidence knowing your property and investment are fully protected throughout the project.
WHAT AREAS DO YOU SERVE AROUND ACWORTH?
We serve Acworth and surrounding communities throughout Cobb County and northwest Georgia, including Kennesaw, Marietta, Woodstock, Canton, Dallas, Smyrna, Cartersville, and more. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, give us a call at (770) 975-5858 and we'll let you know right away.
