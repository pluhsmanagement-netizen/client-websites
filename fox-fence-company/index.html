Common Questions

Questions From
Our Neighbors

Do you offer free estimates in Acworth and nearby areas?
Yes — Fox Fence Company offers free, no-obligation estimates to homeowners and businesses throughout Acworth and the surrounding communities. We'll come out, assess your property, and provide a clear, fair quote with no pressure to commit.
What types of fencing do you install?
We install a wide range of fencing types including wood privacy fences, vinyl fencing, chain link, aluminum, iron, and custom decorative fences. Whether you need a residential backyard fence or a commercial-grade security solution, we have the materials and expertise to get it done right.
How long does a typical fence installation take?
Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days, depending on the length of the fence line, the type of material, and any site conditions. Our experienced crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your yard and schedule, and they always clean up thoroughly before leaving.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Yes, Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. You can have complete confidence knowing your project is being handled by a professional, accountable team — protecting both your property and your peace of mind throughout the entire process.
Do you work with HOA communities in Acworth?
Absolutely. We have experience working with HOA boards throughout Acworth and the greater Cobb and Cherokee County areas. Our office team is happy to assist in coordinating approvals and ensuring your fence design meets your community's guidelines before installation begins.
What areas do you serve beyond Acworth?
In addition to our home base in Acworth, GA, we proudly serve Kennesaw, Marietta, Woodstock, Canton, Cartersville, Dallas, Smyrna, Holly Springs, and many communities throughout northwest Georgia. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, just give us a call at (770) 975-5858 and we'll let you know.
Can you repair my existing fence instead of replacing it?
Yes, we offer complete fence repair services including board replacement, post replacement, gate repair, and storm damage restoration. During your free estimate, we'll honestly assess whether repair or full replacement makes the most sense for your situation — and we'll always recommend what's best for your budget and long-term value.
