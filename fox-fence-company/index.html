FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of fences do you install in Acworth, GA?

Fox Fence Company installs a full range of fence styles including wood privacy, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, ornamental iron, and pool fencing. We also offer fully custom designs for unique applications. Whether it's a backyard privacy fence or a commercial perimeter, we have you covered in the greater Acworth area.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all fence installation and repair projects. One of our knowledgeable representatives will visit your property, assess your needs, and walk you through the best options for your budget and goals. Simply call us or fill out our contact form to get started.

Are you licensed and insured in Georgia?

Absolutely. Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to perform fencing work in the state of Georgia. This protects you as the homeowner and ensures all work is completed to professional standards. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

How long does fence installation typically take?

Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days depending on the size of the project, fence type, and terrain. Our experienced crews work efficiently without sacrificing quality. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your free estimate consultation.

Do you handle HOA fence approvals?

Yes, our office staff has experience working with HOA boards and can help facilitate the approval process. We provide documentation, material samples, and design specs to support your HOA submission. Many of our Acworth customers have successfully worked with us through their HOA approval process.

What areas near Acworth do you serve?

We serve Acworth and many surrounding communities in the greater Atlanta metro area including Kennesaw, Marietta, Woodstock, Canton, Cartersville, Dallas, Smyrna, Powder Springs, and Roswell. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call at (770) 975-5858.
