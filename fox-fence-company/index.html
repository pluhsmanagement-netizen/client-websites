Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What areas do you serve near Acworth, GA?
Fox Fence Company is based in Acworth and proudly serves the surrounding northwest Georgia area including Kennesaw, Marietta, Woodstock, Canton, Cartersville, Dallas, Hiram, Holly Springs, and Smyrna. If you're unsure whether we service your area, give us a call at (770) 975-5858 and we'll be happy to let you know.
How much does fence installation cost in Acworth?
Fence installation costs vary depending on the type of fence (wood, vinyl, aluminum, chain link), the length of the project, and any custom features. We offer free, no-obligation estimates so you'll know exactly what to expect before any work begins. Contact us to schedule your estimate today.
Do I need a permit to install a fence in Acworth, GA?
Permit requirements vary by municipality and HOA in Georgia. In many cases, residential fences require a permit from the city or county depending on height and location. Our team is familiar with local regulations and can help guide you through what's needed for your specific property.
How long does fence installation typically take?
Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days depending on the size of the project. Our experienced crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your property and daily routine. We'll give you a clear timeline when we schedule your installation.
What types of fencing do you install?
We install a full range of fencing including wood, vinyl, aluminum, and chain link fences for both residential and commercial properties. We also offer custom and decorative designs, pool fencing, and more. Whatever your fencing need, our team has the experience to deliver quality results.
Are you licensed and insured in Georgia?
Yes — Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. We carry liability insurance to protect your property throughout the installation process. You can hire us with complete confidence knowing you're working with a legitimate, professional fencing company.
Do you repair existing fences or only install new ones?
We handle both! In addition to full fence installations, we offer comprehensive repair services including general fence repairs, post replacements, storm damage repairs, and gate repairs. If your fence is aging or has been damaged, call us for an honest assessment of whether repair or replacement is the right call.
