Questions

What areas does Fox Fence Company serve near Acworth, GA?
We proudly serve Acworth and surrounding communities throughout North Georgia, including Kennesaw, Marietta, Woodstock, Canton, Dallas, Holly Springs, Cartersville, Smyrna, and Roswell. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call at (770) 975-5858 and we'll be happy to confirm.
How much does fence installation cost in Acworth, GA?
Fence installation costs vary depending on the type of fence, the length, your property's terrain, and any gate requirements. We provide free, no-obligation estimates tailored to your specific project — so you get a fair, transparent price before any work begins. Call us or use our contact form to schedule your free estimate.
Is Fox Fence Company licensed and insured in Georgia?
Yes — Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. We carry liability insurance to protect your property during every project. You can hire us with complete confidence knowing we operate professionally and responsibly.
What types of fences do you install?
We install a wide range of fence types including wood privacy fences, vinyl fences, chain link, aluminum, iron, and custom decorative designs. We also handle pool fencing and commercial fencing projects. Whether you need something classic, modern, or completely custom, our team can make it happen.
Do you repair existing fences, or only install new ones?
We do both! Fox Fence Company offers comprehensive fence repair services including post replacement, panel repair, storm damage repair, and gate repair. If your fence is damaged, leaning, or just getting worn out, our team can assess the situation and recommend the most cost-effective solution — whether that's repair or full replacement.
How long does a typical fence installation take?
Most residential fence installations in Acworth are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the project. Our crews work efficiently and professionally to minimize disruption to your daily routine, while never cutting corners on quality. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate.
