— Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of fencing do you install?

Fox Fence Company installs wood, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, and custom ornamental fencing for both residential and commercial properties. We work with homeowners and businesses throughout Acworth, GA and surrounding communities to find the right material and style for every budget and purpose.

How do I get an estimate?

Simply call us at (770) 975-5858 or fill out our online contact form and one of our representatives will schedule a convenient time to visit your property. Estimates are always free with no obligation, and our team will walk you through your options on-site.

How long does fence installation take?

Most residential fence installations are completed within one to two days depending on the size of the project and the materials selected. Our crews are known for working efficiently without cutting corners — many customers are surprised by how quickly a quality fence goes up.

Are you licensed and insured in Georgia?

Yes — Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. You can have confidence that every installation meets local code requirements and that your property is protected throughout the project from start to finish.

Do you handle HOA fence requirements?

Absolutely. Our team is experienced working with HOA boards and their specific guidelines for fence style, height, materials, and placement. We assist homeowners with documentation and communication so the process stays smooth from approval to installation.

What areas do you serve near Acworth?

In addition to Acworth, we proudly serve Kennesaw, Marietta, Canton, Woodstock, Dallas, Powder Springs, Smyrna, Cartersville, and surrounding Cherokee and Cobb County communities. If you're within the greater Atlanta metro area, contact us to confirm service availability at your address.
