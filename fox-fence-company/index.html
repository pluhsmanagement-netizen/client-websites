How much does fence installation cost in Acworth, GA?
Fence installation costs vary depending on the material, height, linear footage, and complexity of your property. Wood fencing is typically the most affordable option, while custom iron or decorative aluminum fencing sits at the higher end. Fox Fence offers free, no-pressure estimates — call (770) 975-5858 or fill out our contact form and we'll provide a detailed quote tailored to your specific project.
Is Fox Fence Company licensed and insured in Georgia?
Yes. Fox Fence Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. We carry appropriate liability insurance to protect both your property and our crew throughout every project. You can feel confident hiring us knowing you're working with a legitimate, accountable local business.
How long does a typical fence installation take?
Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days, depending on the size of the yard and the type of fence. Our experienced crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine. Larger commercial projects or custom designs may take longer, and we'll give you a realistic timeline during your free estimate consultation.
Do I need a permit to install a fence in Acworth, GA?
Permit requirements for fences in Acworth and surrounding Cobb, Cherokee, and Bartow counties depend on the height, location, and type of fence being installed. In many cases, permits are required for fences over a certain height. Fox Fence is familiar with local codes and regulations, and we can help guide you through the process of determining what's needed for your specific project.
What types of fencing does Fox Fence install and repair?
Fox Fence Company installs and repairs a wide variety of fencing materials including wood, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, and iron.
