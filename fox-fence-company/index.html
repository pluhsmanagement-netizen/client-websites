— Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of fences do you install in Acworth, GA?

Fox Fence Company installs a wide range of fencing types throughout Acworth and the surrounding communities, including wood privacy fences, vinyl fences, chain link, aluminum, and custom decorative designs. We also handle pool enclosures and commercial perimeter fencing for properties of any size.

How much does a fence installation cost?

Fence pricing depends on material, height, linear footage, terrain conditions, and any gate or custom features you require. We provide free on-site estimates so you receive an accurate, no-surprise quote tailored to your specific property before any work begins.

Do you work with homeowner associations (HOAs)?

Yes — our office team is experienced in coordinating with HOA boards to ensure your fence design and materials meet community guidelines. We can help you navigate the approval process and recommend styles that are likely to be approved without delays.

How long does a typical fence installation take?

Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days depending on the size of the property and material selected. Our crews are known for working efficiently and thoroughly, and they always clean up the job site completely before leaving.

Do you repair existing fences, or only install new ones?

We handle both installation and repair, including storm damage repair, post replacement, gate repair, and general fence restoration across all material types. If you are unsure whether your fence needs a full replacement or just targeted repairs, we are happy to give you an honest assessment during our free estimate visit.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?

Yes, Fox Fence Company is fully licensed to operate in the state of Georgia and carries comprehensive liability insurance. You can have complete confidence that your property and our workers are protected throughout the duration of every project we undertake.
