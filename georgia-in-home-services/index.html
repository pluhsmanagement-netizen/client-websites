Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a full roof replacement or just a repair?
A thorough inspection by our licensed team will assess the age, condition, and extent of damage to your roof. If less than 30% of the shingles are affected and the deck is sound, a repair may suffice — otherwise, a full replacement is more cost-effective long-term.
Does Georgia In Home Services work with insurance companies for storm damage claims?
Yes — we have extensive experience working directly with insurance adjusters to help Atlanta homeowners navigate claims for storm, water, and fire damage. Our team documents all damage thoroughly and advocates on your behalf to maximize your claim and minimize out-of-pocket costs.
What areas of Atlanta do you serve?
We serve Atlanta and surrounding communities throughout the metro area, including Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Smyrna, Roswell, Alpharetta, Tucker, and Dunwoody. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, give us a call at (404) 434-2436 and we'll be happy to confirm.
Are you really available 24/7 for water and fire emergencies?
Absolutely. Water and fire damage don't keep business hours, and neither do we. Call (404) 434-2436 any time — day or night — and our emergency response team will mobilize quickly to secure your property and begin the restoration process.
What roofing materials do you install, and do they come with a warranty?
As a GAF Master Elite contractor, we install industry-leading roofing systems including lifetime warranty architectural shingles. We offer a range of products to suit different budgets and styles, and all installations are backed by both manufacturer and workmanship warranties.
How long does a typical remodeling or roofing project take to complete?
Project timelines vary depending on scope and materials — a standard roof replacement is often completed in one to two days, while kitchen or bathroom remodels may take two to six weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule upfront and communicate any changes promptly so you're never left wondering.
