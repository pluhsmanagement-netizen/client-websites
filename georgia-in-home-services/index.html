About Us

Built on Honesty.
Delivered with Skill.

Established in 1996, Georgia In Home Services is a licensed, bonded, and insured general contractor serving the greater Atlanta area. Our home improvement services span the full spectrum — from roofing and siding to complete kitchen and bathroom remodels, deck building, painting, new construction, and full-scale restoration after water or fire damage.

"We treat your property as if it were our own — every project, every time."

What sets our team apart is transparency. No hidden costs, no surprise invoices — just clear communication, honest estimates, and workmanship that holds up for decades. Whether you're navigating an insurance claim after storm damage or planning a dream kitchen renovation, our team is with you from the first call to the final walk-through.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer 24/7 emergency services for water and fire damage?
Yes — our emergency response team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for water and fire damage situations. When you call (404) 434-2436, a live team member will respond immediately and dispatch a crew to secure and assess your property as quickly as possible. Fast response limits further damage and protects your insurance claim.
Are you licensed, bonded, and insured in Georgia?
Absolutely. Georgia In Home Services is fully licensed, bonded, and insured as a general contractor in the state of Georgia. We carry all required coverages to protect both our workers and your property throughout every project. You can request proof of insurance and licensing at any time before work begins.
How does your insurance claims process work for roofing?
Our team has extensive experience working directly with insurance adjusters on storm and water damage claims. We'll document the damage thoroughly, meet with your adjuster on-site, and advocate for a fair, comprehensive settlement — not just for the roof but for any related interior repairs as well. Clients frequently receive more coverage than they expected when we're involved in the process.
What roofing materials do you install?
We install a wide range of roofing systems including architectural asphalt shingles (including lifetime-warranty products), metal roofing, flat/TPO systems, and impact-resistant options. As a GAF Master Elite certified contractor, we have access to the industry's strongest material warranties and can help you select the right system for Atlanta's climate and your home's architecture.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta can be completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We schedule projects to minimize disruption to your daily life, and our crews work efficiently without cutting corners on material installation or cleanup. We'll provide a clear timeline before the project begins.
Do you handle both roofing and interior remodeling on the same project?
Yes — this is one of our key differentiators. As a full-service general contractor, we can manage roofing and all related interior repairs (drywall, paint, flooring, structural elements) under a single contract. This simplifies your project management, protects quality continuity, and often accelerates the overall timeline versus hiring multiple specialty contractors.
