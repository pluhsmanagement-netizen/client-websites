Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas of Atlanta do you serve?
Georgia In Home Services proudly serves Atlanta and the greater metro area including Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Smyrna, Dunwoody, Roswell, Alpharetta, and Tucker. We're based at 2625 Piedmont Rd NE in Atlanta and serve homeowners across a wide radius. Call us at (404) 434-2436 to confirm service in your area.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency roofing and restoration services?
Yes. We are a 24-hour immediate emergency responder for water and fire damage situations. Whether a storm tears shingles from your roof at midnight or a pipe bursts and floods multiple floors, our team mobilizes quickly to protect your Atlanta home and minimize damage.
How does the insurance claim process work for storm or fire damage?
Our team has extensive experience navigating insurance claims alongside Atlanta homeowners. We'll document all damage, work directly with your adjuster, and help you maximize your claim — not just for the visible damage, but for all related repairs. Many of our clients have achieved fully covered claims because of our thorough approach.
Are you licensed, bonded, and insured in Georgia?
Absolutely. Georgia In Home Services is a fully licensed, bonded, and insured general contractor operating in Georgia. We are also IICRC certified for water and fire restoration, and a GAF Master Elite roofing contractor — ensuring your project meets the highest professional and safety standards.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta take one to three days depending on the size of the home, the materials selected, and weather conditions. We work efficiently without cutting corners, and our crews clean up thoroughly at the end of each work day so your property remains presentable throughout the process.
Can you handle both roofing and interior remodeling on the same project?
Yes — and this is one of our core strengths. As a full-service contractor, we handle everything from roof replacement to kitchen renovations, bathroom remodels, painting, siding, and restoration under one roof. This means you work with a single trusted team, reducing coordination headaches and ensuring consistent quality across your entire project.
