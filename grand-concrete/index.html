About Grand Concrete

Built on Precision.
Grounded in Trust.

Grand Concrete is a full-service concrete and construction company based in Stillwater, MN, dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship on every residential and commercial project we undertake. From patios and driveways to structural foundations and specialty concrete work, our team brings meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

"Every project is personal — we treat your property with the same care we'd give our own."

With deep roots in the Stillwater community and a reputation built entirely on referrals and five-star reviews, Grand Concrete stands as the region's most trusted name in concrete and construction services. Our owner Andy and his crew bring transparency, flexibility, and genuine pride to every pour, every project, every time.
