FAQ

Common Questions.

How do I know if my roof needs to be replaced or just repaired?
If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, repair is often the right move. However, widespread granule loss, sagging decking, multiple leak points, or a roof over 20 years old typically warrant full replacement. ImproveIT! of Atlanta provides honest inspections that give you the complete picture — we'll always recommend the option that makes financial sense for your home.
Does ImproveIT! of Atlanta work with insurance companies for storm damage claims?
Yes — we regularly work alongside homeowners and their insurance carriers throughout the claims process. Our team documents storm damage thoroughly, provides detailed estimates in insurance-compatible formats, and can communicate directly with your adjuster. We've helped hundreds of Atlanta homeowners navigate the claims process without added stress.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of your home's roof system. We schedule your project around favorable weather and provide you with a timeline before work begins. You'll always know what to expect — no surprises.
What roofing materials do you recommend for Atlanta's climate?
Atlanta's combination of summer heat, humidity, and occasional severe weather makes impact-resistant architectural shingles our most recommended option for most homes. Metal roofing is an excellent long-term investment as well, offering superior heat reflection and storm resistance. We'll walk you through material options and manufacturer warranties so you can make a confident choice.
Is ImproveIT! of Atlanta licensed and insured in Georgia?
Yes. ImproveIT! of Atlanta operates as a fully licensed and insured roofing contractor in the state of Georgia. We carry both general liability and workers' compensation coverage, which protects you as a homeowner. We're happy to provide proof of insurance before any project begins.
Do you offer any warranty on your roofing work?
Every roofing project we complete is backed by both a manufacturer's material warranty and our own workmanship warranty. The specifics vary by material type and project scope — your project manager will walk you through what's covered before installation begins. Our commitment to quality means we stand behind every roof we install.
