GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Atlanta homeowners ask us most. Don't see your question? Call us — we're always happy to chat.

How do I know if I need a roof replacement or just a repair?
If your roof is under 15 years old and damage is localized to one area, a repair is usually sufficient. However, if you're seeing widespread shingle loss, significant storm damage, or an aging roof over 20 years old, replacement is often the smarter long-term investment. We offer free inspections to give you an honest recommendation — no pressure, no upsell.
Do you work with homeowners' insurance for storm damage claims?
Yes — we work with homeowners through the insurance claim process regularly. Our team documents all storm damage thoroughly with photos and a detailed report to support your claim. We communicate directly with adjusters when needed to make sure you get a fair settlement and your roof gets properly repaired.
Which Atlanta-area neighborhoods and cities do you serve?
We proudly serve Atlanta and the surrounding metro area including Buckhead, Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Smyrna, Roswell, Alpharetta, Dunwoody, Brookhaven, and more. If you're within 30 miles of Atlanta, chances are we serve your neighborhood — give us a call to confirm your area.
How long does a roof replacement typically take?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. We work efficiently and clean up completely before we leave so you're not inconvenienced any longer than necessary. Weather and material availability may occasionally affect scheduling, and we'll always keep you informed.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Absolutely — ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing work in the state of Georgia. This protects you as a homeowner throughout the entire project. We're happy to provide documentation before work begins so you have complete peace of mind.
What type of warranty do you offer on roofing work?
We stand behind our workmanship with a contractor warranty on all installations, and most materials we install carry manufacturer warranties as well. The exact terms depend on the product selected and the scope of your project. We'll walk you through all warranty details clearly before any work begins.
