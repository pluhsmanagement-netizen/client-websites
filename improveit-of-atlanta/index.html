Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers from your Atlanta roofing neighbors at ImproveIT!.

How much does a roof replacement cost in Atlanta?
Roof replacement costs in Atlanta typically range from $7,000 to $20,000+, depending on your home's square footage, roof pitch, material choice, and the extent of any underlying damage. ImproveIT! of Atlanta provides free, no-obligation estimates so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.
Do you serve my neighborhood in the Atlanta metro area?
ImproveIT! of Atlanta proudly serves homeowners across the greater Atlanta area including Buckhead, Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Smyrna, Roswell, Alpharetta, Dunwoody, Johns Creek, Norcross, and Tucker. If you're unsure whether we cover your zip code, simply call us at (678) 498-5075 and we'll confirm immediately.
Will you help me with my insurance claim after storm damage?
Absolutely. Atlanta's severe weather season can leave homeowners overwhelmed dealing with insurance companies. Our team has extensive experience working alongside insurance adjusters — we document the damage thoroughly, provide detailed estimates, and communicate directly with your insurer to streamline the claims process on your behalf.
How long does a roof replacement typically take?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. Our crews work efficiently and clean up thoroughly each day, minimizing disruption to your home and family. We'll give you a clear timeline before work begins.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Yes. ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed in the state of Georgia and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We are happy to provide proof of insurance upon request so you can hire with complete peace of mind.
Do you offer any warranty on your roofing work?
Yes — we stand behind our workmanship with a labor warranty in addition to the manufacturer's material warranty on shingles and roofing products. We want every Atlanta homeowner to feel confident that their investment is protected long after installation day.
What roofing materials work best for Atlanta's climate?
Atlanta's climate — hot summers, high humidity, and occasional severe storms — calls for durable, weather-resistant materials. We recommend architectural asphalt shingles for budget-friendly durability, or metal roofing for superior longevity and energy efficiency. Our inspectors will walk you through the best options for your specific home and budget.
