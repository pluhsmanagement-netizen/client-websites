Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my roof needs to be replaced or just repaired?
If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, a targeted repair is often the right call. However, widespread shingle deterioration, multiple leaks, or storm damage affecting large sections typically make a full replacement more cost-effective in the long run. Our free inspection will give you an honest assessment of your best options.
Does ImproveIT! of Atlanta work with homeowners insurance for storm damage?
Yes — we have extensive experience guiding Atlanta homeowners through the insurance claims process for storm and hail damage. We document damage thoroughly, communicate directly with your adjuster, and ensure nothing is overlooked so you receive the coverage you're entitled to.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta are completed in one to two days, depending on roof size, pitch, and weather conditions. We coordinate scheduling to minimize disruption and always leave your property clean and debris-free when we're done.
What roofing materials do you recommend for Atlanta's climate?
Georgia's heat, humidity, and frequent storm activity make impact-resistant architectural shingles and metal roofing excellent choices for durability and energy performance. We'll walk you through the pros and cons of each option based on your home's structure, budget, and aesthetic goals.
Is ImproveIT! of Atlanta licensed and insured?
Absolutely. ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing work throughout the state of Georgia. We carry both general liability and workers' compensation coverage, protecting you as the homeowner from any on-site liability.
Do you offer any warranty on your roofing work?
Yes — we stand behind every project with a workmanship warranty in addition to the manufacturer's material warranties, which can range from 25 years to lifetime coverage depending on the product selected. We'll explain all warranty details clearly before your project begins.
