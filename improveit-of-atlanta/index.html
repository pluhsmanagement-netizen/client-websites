Questions Answered

Frequently
Asked

How do I know if I need a full roof replacement or just a repair?
The decision depends on the age of your roof, the extent of damage, and the cost comparison between repair and replacement. Roofs older than 15-20 years with widespread damage are typically better candidates for full replacement, while isolated issues on newer roofs may be effectively repaired. Our free inspection provides an honest assessment with no obligation to commit.
Does ImproveIT! of Atlanta work with homeowners' insurance for storm damage?
Absolutely — insurance claim navigation is one of our core strengths. We document all storm damage thoroughly, communicate directly with your adjuster, and ensure your claim reflects the true scope of damage to your property. Atlanta homeowners frequently discover additional coverages they were unaware of through our claims process.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in the Atlanta area are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and pitch of the roof and the materials selected. Our crews are efficient without sacrificing quality, and we provide daily updates so you always know exactly where your project stands.
What roofing materials do you recommend for Atlanta's climate?
Atlanta's humid subtropical climate demands materials that resist moisture, algae growth, and high winds. We most frequently recommend architectural asphalt shingles with algae-resistant coatings for value-conscious homeowners, and standing seam metal roofing for maximum longevity. We'll walk you through all options during your consultation based on your budget and goals.
Is ImproveIT! of Atlanta licensed and insured?
Yes — ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed and carries comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance. This protects you as a homeowner from any liability during the project. We are happy to provide insurance documentation upon request before any work begins.
Do you offer any warranty on your roofing work?
We stand fully behind our workmanship with a labor warranty on all installations, backed by manufacturer warranties on the materials we install. Depending on the product line selected, material warranties can extend significantly — providing Atlanta homeowners long-term peace of mind. Warranty details are provided in writing with every completed project.
