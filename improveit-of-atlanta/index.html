Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a full roof replacement or just a repair?

The best way to determine whether you need a repair or a full replacement is a professional roof inspection. Generally, if your roof is nearing the end of its expected lifespan (20+ years for asphalt shingles), has widespread damage, or has had repeated leaks, a replacement is often the more cost-effective long-term solution. Our team provides honest assessments with no pressure.

How long does a roof replacement take in Atlanta?

Most residential roof replacements in the Atlanta area are completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. Weather conditions and material availability can affect the timeline. Our team communicates every step of the schedule clearly so you always know what to expect.

Does ImproveIT! of Atlanta help with insurance claims for storm damage?

Yes. We have extensive experience working alongside Atlanta homeowners and their insurance providers on storm damage claims. We document all damage thoroughly during inspection and can work directly with your adjuster to help ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to. We guide you through every stage of the process.

What roofing materials do you recommend for Atlanta's climate?

Atlanta's humid subtropical climate, with hot summers and occasional severe storms, calls for roofing materials that handle heat, humidity, and wind well. We commonly recommend high-quality asphalt shingles with impact-resistance ratings, as well as metal roofing for its longevity and energy efficiency. During our free estimate, we'll discuss the best option for your specific home and budget.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?

Yes, ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing work throughout the state of Georgia. Our crews carry liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage, protecting both our team and your property throughout every project.

Do you offer any warranty on your roofing work?

We stand behind the quality of our installations. Our projects come with both manufacturer warranties on the roofing materials and a workmanship warranty on our labor. Warranty specifics vary by project and material selected — our team will walk you through full warranty details during your consultation.
