Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers from your Atlanta roofing neighbors.

How do I know if my Atlanta home needs a full roof replacement or just repairs?
If your roof is under 15 years old and the damage is localized, repairs may be all you need. However, if shingles are curling, granules are filling your gutters, or you have multiple leak points, a full replacement is often the smarter long-term investment — especially given Atlanta's humid climate and storm seasons. We'll give you an honest assessment during your free inspection with no pressure to choose the more expensive option.
What areas near Atlanta does ImproveIT! of Atlanta serve?
We proudly serve Atlanta and the surrounding metro area, including Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Alpharetta, Smyrna, Kennesaw, Roswell, Dunwoody, Brookhaven, and Peachtree City. If you're not sure whether we cover your neighborhood, just give us a call at (678) 498-5075 and we'll let you know right away.
How long does a typical roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta can be completed in one to two days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while never cutting corners on quality or safety. You'll receive a clear project timeline before any work begins.
Do you assist with insurance claims for storm damage in Georgia?
Yes — navigating insurance claims can be stressful, and we're here to help. Our team will document the storm damage thoroughly with photos and a detailed report that your insurance adjuster will need. We work alongside you throughout the claims process to help ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to under your Georgia homeowner's policy.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Absolutely. ImproveIT! of Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing work in the state of Georgia. This protects both your home and our team on every project. We're happy to provide proof of insurance and licensing documentation upon request before any work begins.
What roofing materials do you recommend for Atlanta's climate?
Atlanta's hot summers, occasional ice storms, and heavy spring rains call for durable, moisture-resistant roofing materials. We most commonly recommend architectural asphalt shingles for their proven performance and value in Georgia's climate. For homeowners wanting superior longevity and energy efficiency, standing seam metal roofing is an excellent long-term investment. We'll walk you through all options and help you choose what's right for your home and budget.
