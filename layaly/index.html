Our Story

A table set in tradition

Layaly was born from a simple devotion: to bring the soul of Mediterranean cooking — its olive oils, its slow-simmered stews, its bright herbs and warm spices — to a single, intimate table.

Every plate is built on family recipes carried across generations and the freshest seasonal produce. From hand-rolled grape leaves to fire-grilled kababs and signature knafeh, we cook the way our grandmothers did: with patience, with care, and with love.

Welcome to Layaly

Meet the Owner

Paul Jaraysi

Owner · Layaly Mediterranean

Layaly is more than just a restaurant — it's a story of family, resilience, and relentless learning. My dad started Layaly in 2021 after dreaming of opening a restaurant since 2012. By 2023, it was failing. I made the decision to step in and take over, even though I had zero restaurant experience — and had just quit my real estate career to do it.

It wasn't easy. There were moments I seriously wanted to quit. But I committed. I studied the business, visited competitors, observed everything, and constantly adapted until something finally worked.

What sets Layaly apart is the passion and energy we pour into the space every single day. We're not just serving food — we're sharing dishes our people grew up on. Guests often tell us the food makes them feel emotional, like they're back at their grandmother's table or home for the holidays. That kind of connection is rare, and we don't take it lightly.

"We lead with heart. Layaly is warm, authentic, and alive with culture."
