Our Story

A table set in tradition

Layaly was born from a simple devotion: to bring the soul of Mediterranean cooking — its olive oils, its slow-simmered stews, its bright herbs and warm spices — to a single, intimate table.

Every plate is built on family recipes carried across generations and the freshest seasonal produce. From hand-rolled grape leaves to fire-grilled kababs and signature knafeh, we cook the way our grandmothers did: with patience, with care, and with love.
