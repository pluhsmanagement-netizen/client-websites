FAQ

Common Questions,
Honest Answers

Do you offer free estimates for home services in Atlanta?

Yes — all consultations and initial estimates are completely free with no obligation. We believe in transparency from the very first conversation, so you'll receive a clear, itemized quote before any work begins. Call us at 033 822 40 40 to schedule yours.

What areas of Atlanta do you serve?

We serve Atlanta and all surrounding communities including Marietta, Decatur, Sandy Springs, Smyrna, Roswell, Alpharetta, Norcross, and beyond. If you're within 30 miles of Atlanta, there's a good chance we serve your neighborhood — call us to confirm coverage in your area.

Are you licensed and insured in Georgia?

Absolutely. Layaly is fully licensed to operate in the state of Georgia and carries comprehensive liability insurance on every job. We carry documentation you can request before any work begins. Your home is protected — always.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency service call?

We're open seven days a week from 11AM to 10PM and respond to emergency requests as quickly as possible. For urgent situations, calling 033 822 40 40 directly gives you the fastest path to getting a technician dispatched to your location.

Do you offer maintenance plans for ongoing home care?

Yes. Our maintenance plans are designed for homeowners who want proactive care rather than reactive repairs. We schedule regular inspections and service visits so small issues are caught early — saving you money and stress in the long run. Ask us for plan details when you call.

What is your warranty policy on completed work?

Every project completed by Layaly is backed by our workmanship warranty. If something we installed or repaired isn't performing as expected within the warranty period, we come back and make it right — at no additional cost to you. Coverage details are provided in writing before we begin.
