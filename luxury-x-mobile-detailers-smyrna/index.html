FAQ

Common questions.

Q.01

What areas do you serve in the Atlanta metro?

A.01

We serve Smyrna, Atlanta, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Hiram, Douglasville, and the surrounding communities. We are fully mobile, so the team comes directly to your home or office.

Q.02

How long does a full detail service take?

A.02

A standard full detail runs 3 to 6 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. Paint correction services can take longer. We recently spent 5.5+ hours on a single car to get every clear coat scratch corrected. We never rush the process.

Q.03

What is ceramic coating and why should I consider it?

A.03

Ceramic coating is an advanced liquid polymer that bonds chemically with your paint, creating a long lasting hydrophobic shield. Unlike traditional wax it can last years rather than months, repelling water, UV, and contaminants while keeping a deep gloss.

Q.04

Do I need to be home when you detail my car?

A.04

Not necessarily. Many clients leave keys with us while they are at work or running errands. We just need access to the vehicle and a reasonable amount of space to work safely. We keep you updated on progress throughout.
