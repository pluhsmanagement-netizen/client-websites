FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know about our auto detailing services in Smyrna and the Atlanta metro area.

Call Us Now

What areas do you serve in the Atlanta metro?
We serve Smyrna, Atlanta, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Hiram, Douglasville, and surrounding communities across the greater Atlanta metro area. We're a fully mobile service — our team comes directly to your location.

What is ceramic coating and why should I consider it?
Ceramic coating is an advanced liquid polymer that bonds chemically with your vehicle's paint, creating a long-lasting hydrophobic layer of protection. Unlike traditional wax, it can last years rather than months, repelling water, UV rays, and chemical contaminants while maintaining an intense gloss.

How long does a full detail service typically take?
A standard full detail typically takes 3–6 hours depending on the size of the vehicle and its condition. Paint correction services can take longer — our team spent 5.5+ hours on one vehicle recently to get every scratch corrected properly. We never rush the process.

Will detailing remove scratches and paint defects?
Our paint correction service addresses swirl marks, light scratches, oxidation, and clear coat defects through multi-stage machine polishing. We can remove the vast majority of surface-level defects. Deep scratches that penetrate the clear coat may require repainting rather than correction.

Do I need to be home when you detail my car?
Not necessarily — many clients prefer to leave their keys with us while they're at work or running errands. We simply need access to the vehicle and a reasonable amount of space to work safely. We'll communicate with you throughout and ensure you're updated on progress.

How do I get a quote for detailing services?
The fastest way to get a quote is to call or text us at (770) 764-6393. We'll ask a few questions about your vehicle's make, model, condition, and the services you're interested in, then provide a transparent price — no hidden fees. You can also use our contact form below.
