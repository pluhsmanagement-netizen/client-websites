FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a concrete driveway or patio installation take?

Most residential driveway or patio pours are completed within one to two days, depending on size and complexity. Curing takes an additional 24–48 hours before light foot traffic, and full cure strength is reached within 28 days.

What's the difference between regular and stamped concrete?

Standard concrete is poured and finished smooth or with a broom texture. Stamped concrete uses impression molds and integral color to mimic materials like stone, brick, or slate. Stamped finishes cost more but dramatically improve curb appeal.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all concrete projects in the atlanta area. Simply call us at (678) 252-9536 or submit the contact form on this page and we'll be in touch promptly.

How do I know if my concrete needs repair or full replacement?

Surface-level cracks, minor spalling, and discoloration are typically good candidates for repair or resurfacing. Deep structural cracks, widespread sinking, or severely deteriorated slabs may require full replacement. Our team will give you an honest assessment during your free estimate.

What areas do you serve around Atlanta?

We proudly serve Atlanta and the surrounding metro, including Marietta, Smyrna, Decatur, Sandy Springs, Roswell, Alpharetta, Dunwoody, College Park, and Norcross. Not sure if we cover your location? Give us a call and we'll let you know.

How should I maintain my concrete after installation?

We recommend sealing your concrete every 2–3 years to protect against moisture, oil, and weathering. Regular cleaning with a mild detergent and avoiding de-icing salts in winter will extend the life of your slab significantly. We're happy to advise on maintenance after your project is complete.
