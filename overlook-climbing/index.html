About Us

BUILT FOR CLIMBERS,
By Climbers

Overlook Climbing is Atlanta's dedicated indoor rock climbing facility, nestled right on the iconic BeltLine — where community, fitness, and vertical adventure converge for climbers of every skill level. From first-timers to seasoned athletes, we've built an environment that challenges and inspires.

We offer world-class climbing programs, youth camps, fitness classes, and private instruction — all designed to foster skill development, community connection, and a lifelong love of climbing in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia.

FAQ

COMMON
Questions

Do I need experience to visit Overlook Climbing?
Not at all — Overlook Climbing welcomes climbers of every skill level, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Our facility has routes for all grades, and our staff is always available to help newcomers get started safely and confidently. First-timers are encouraged to try our intro sessions designed specifically to build foundational skills.
What are your hours of operation?
We're open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including holidays. Whether you prefer an early morning session before work or a late-night climb after dinner, Overlook Climbing is always ready for you. Special events and private bookings may have specific time windows; check our calendar for details.
Do you offer youth climbing programs and summer camps?
Yes! We offer a full range of youth programs including climbing teams, weekly classes, and our popular Camp Overlook summer camp. Our youth programs are designed to build strength, confidence, and problem-solving skills in a fun, safe environment. Summer camp registration opens each spring — check our website for current availability.
Can I rent climbing gear at your facility?
Absolutely. Overlook Climbing offers crash pad rentals and full equipment packages so you can climb without needing to invest in your own gear right away. All rental equipment is regularly inspected and maintained to the highest safety standards. Our staff will help you select the right gear for your session.
How do I sign up for a membership or private instruction?
You can sign up for a membership or book private 1:1 instruction directly through our customer portal on the website, or by contacting our team in person or via our contact form. Membership options include monthly and annual plans with different access levels. Private instruction is bookable in single or multi-session packages.
Do you host birthday parties and private events?
Yes — Overlook Climbing is an incredible venue for youth birthday parties, corporate team-building, and private group events. We offer customizable packages that include facility access, instruction, and equipment rental. Contact us to discuss availability and book your special event with our dedicated events team.
