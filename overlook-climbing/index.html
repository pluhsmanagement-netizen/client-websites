/01 — About Us Built for climbers

Atlanta's
climbing
home.

The Overlook is a climbing community on Atlanta's BeltLine — a place where first-timers and seasoned crushers share the same mats, the same chalk, and the same problems on the wall.

We run climbing classes, youth teams, summer camps, fitness sessions, and private instruction. Whatever brought you in, we'll help you stay.

"Every hold is a question. Every climb is the answer you weren't expecting."

/04 — Mission What we stand for

Climbing,
community
& connection.

The Overlook was founded on three principles. We love to climb and we live to climb, and we're dedicated to making climbing accessible to everyone — first-timers and seasoned crushers alike. Climbing is the foundation, but community is the heart — an all-inclusive space where members are empowered to use their strengths to reach collective goals, across race, age, sex, orientation, ability, and background.

Through our passion for the sport and our focus on people, we connect climbers through excellence in route setting, instruction, creative programs, outreach, and safety. The goal: a place where Atlanta's beautifully diverse cultures come together as one.
