FAQ

Common Roofing Questions

How quickly can Porter & Sons Roofing respond to an emergency?
We offer same day or next day roofing services for emergency situations throughout the Atlanta area. Our team is available Monday through Saturday around the clock, so whether a storm hits overnight or you discover a leak on a weekend morning, we'll be there fast to assess and address the damage before it gets worse.

Is Porter & Sons Roofing LLC licensed and insured in Georgia?
Yes, Porter & Sons Roofing LLC is fully bonded, insured, and licensed to operate in the state of Georgia. We take compliance and safety seriously on every job site, protecting both our crew and your property. You can hire us with complete confidence knowing you're working with a legitimate, professional roofing company.

What types of roofing services do you provide in Atlanta?
We offer a comprehensive range of roofing services including emergency roof repairs, full roof replacements, shingle installation, storm damage restoration, hail damage repair, leak repairs, skylight installation, ventilation upgrades, and routine maintenance plans. When it comes to roofs, we do it all — serving homeowners throughout the Atlanta metro area.

Can you help me with an insurance claim for storm or hail damage?
Absolutely. Our team has extensive experience working with insurance companies to document storm and hail damage, communicate with adjusters, and ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to. We handle the paperwork and photos so you don't have to navigate the process alone, making the entire claim and repair experience as smooth as possible.

How much does a roof repair or replacement cost in Atlanta?
Roofing costs vary based on the size of your home, materials chosen, and the extent of damage or replacement needed. We provide free, no-obligation estimates and are committed to competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our customers consistently note our quotes come in lower than expected — call us at (470) 400-0059 and we'll get you an honest number quickly.

What areas near Atlanta do you serve?
We proudly serve Atlanta and the surrounding metro area, including Decatur, Marietta, Smyrna, Roswell, Sandy Springs, Alpharetta, Norcross, Duluth, and Kennesaw. If you're within the greater Atlanta region and need roofing help, chances are we cover your area. Call us to confirm coverage for your specific location.
