FAQ

Common questions answered.

Do you offer free estimates for home services in Atlanta?

Yes — we provide complimentary estimates for all home services projects in Atlanta and the surrounding metro area. Simply call or submit the contact form and we'll schedule a convenient time to assess your property and discuss your needs.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?

Absolutely. pressure is fully licensed and insured in the state of Georgia. We carry all required documentation for residential home services and can provide proof of coverage upon request before any work begins.

What areas near Atlanta do you serve?

We serve Atlanta and a wide range of surrounding communities including Decatur, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Smyrna, Roswell, Alpharetta, Norcross, and Kennesaw. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, give us a call and we'll confirm quickly.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency service call?

For emergency situations, we prioritize same-day response throughout the Atlanta metro. Contact us directly by phone to reach our emergency line and we'll dispatch a technician as quickly as possible based on your location.

What does your maintenance plan include?

Our maintenance plans are customized to your property's specific systems and age. They typically include scheduled inspections, preventative servicing, priority emergency response, and discounted rates on any additional repairs identified during visits.

Do you offer any warranty on your work?

Yes — all completed projects are backed by our workmanship warranty. If any issue arises directly related to our installation or repair work within the warranty period, we return at no additional cost to make it right. Details are provided in your project agreement.
