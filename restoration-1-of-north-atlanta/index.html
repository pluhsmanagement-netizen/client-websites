Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you offer 24/7 emergency restoration services in Alpharetta?

Yes — Restoration 1 of North Atlanta is available around the clock, every day of the year, including holidays. Emergency situations like water damage, flood, or structural failure don't wait for business hours, and neither do we. Call (678) 390-2145 anytime for immediate response.

What types of water damage can you remediate?

We handle all categories of water damage — from minor pipe leaks (Category 1) to grey water incidents (Category 2) and severely contaminated water such as sewage backups (Category 3). Our certified technicians assess the situation thoroughly and execute complete drying, sanitization, and structural restoration protocols.

Will you work directly with my insurance company?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with major insurance carriers and can help facilitate the claims process on your behalf. Our team provides detailed documentation, damage assessments, and itemized estimates that meet insurance requirements, reducing your stress during an already difficult time.

How long does a typical restoration or remodeling project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on the scope and type of work. Emergency drying and remediation may be complete in 3–7 days, while full construction or remodeling projects can range from a few weeks to several months. We provide a clear project schedule during your free estimate so you always know what to expect.

Are you licensed and insured to operate in Georgia?

Yes. Restoration 1 of North Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform construction and restoration work in the state of Georgia. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both our clients and our team on every project.

What areas around Alpharetta do you serve?

We proudly serve Alpharetta and a wide range of communities throughout North Atlanta, including Johns Creek, Roswell, Milton, Cumming, Duluth, Suwanee, Marietta, Canton, and Woodstock. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, simply call us at (678) 390-2145 and we'll confirm right away.

Do you offer free estimates for construction and restoration projects?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site estimates for all projects. Our team will visit your property, assess the scope of work, and provide transparent, itemized pricing with no hidden fees. We believe in straightforward communication so you can make informed decisions with complete confidence.
