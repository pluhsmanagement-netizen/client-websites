Have Questions?

Frequently Asked

What types of restoration services do you offer in Alpharetta, GA?
We specialize in water damage restoration, fire and smoke damage restoration, mold remediation, and storm damage repair. Beyond emergency remediation, we also provide full reconstruction services including general contracting, remodeling, and specialty build-outs throughout the Alpharetta and North Atlanta area.
How quickly can you respond to a water or fire emergency?
We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including weekends and holidays. Our team aims to be on-site as quickly as possible following your call, typically within hours of first contact. Fast response minimizes secondary damage and reduces overall restoration costs.
Do you work directly with insurance companies?
Yes — we have extensive experience working alongside homeowners and their insurance adjusters through the claims process. We document damage thoroughly, provide detailed estimates, and communicate directly with your insurer to help ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to.
Are you licensed and insured in Georgia?
Absolutely. Restoration 1 of North Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Georgia. We carry the required general liability and workers' compensation coverage, so you're protected throughout every phase of your restoration or construction project.
How do you communicate with homeowners during a project?
Our team keeps clients informed through regular calls, texts, and on-site walkthroughs at key project milestones. We believe transparency is essential — you'll always know what's happening, what comes next, and what it will cost before any work proceeds.
Which cities near Alpharetta do you serve?
We proudly serve Alpharetta and the broader North Atlanta metro area including Roswell, Johns Creek, Milton, Cumming, Canton, Duluth, Suwanee, Dawsonville, and Woodstock. If you're unsure whether we serve your area, give us a call — we're happy to help.
