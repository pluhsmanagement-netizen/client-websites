About Us

North Atlanta's Trusted Restoration Experts

Restoration 1 of North Atlanta is a locally operated construction and restoration company serving Alpharetta, GA and surrounding communities with expert water damage remediation, structural repairs, and full-scale rebuilds. Our team brings a hands-on, detail-driven approach to every project — from emergency water extraction to finished renovations.

We pride ourselves on transparent communication, rapid response, and exceptional results that restore both properties and peace of mind. Whether it's a burst pipe, storm damage, or a planned renovation, we treat every home and business as if it were our own.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of damage does Restoration 1 of North Atlanta handle?
We handle water damage, fire and smoke damage, mold remediation, storm damage, and sewer backup cleanup. Our team is equipped to respond to both residential and commercial properties throughout the Alpharetta area. We also provide full reconstruction services once remediation is complete.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency response in Alpharetta, GA?
Yes, we are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — including holidays. When disaster strikes, a rapid response is critical to minimizing damage, so we prioritize getting to your property as quickly as possible. Call us anytime at (678) 390-2145.
Will you work directly with my insurance company?
Absolutely. We have extensive experience working alongside insurance adjusters and can help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and communicate directly with your insurer to help ensure your claim is handled properly. Our goal is to reduce the stress on you during an already difficult time.
How long does a typical water damage restoration take?
The timeline varies based on the extent of damage, but most drying and remediation phases take between 3 to 7 days. Full reconstruction afterward can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks depending on the scope of work. We'll give you a clear project timeline during our initial assessment.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Yes, Restoration 1 of North Atlanta is fully licensed and insured to perform construction and restoration work in the state of Georgia. We maintain comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect our clients and our team on every project.
What areas do you serve around Alpharetta?
We proudly serve Alpharetta and surrounding communities throughout North Atlanta including Roswell, Johns Creek, Cumming, Milton, Duluth, Suwanee, Sandy Springs, Peachtree City, and Marietta. If you're unsure whether we serve your location, give us a call and we'll be happy to confirm.
