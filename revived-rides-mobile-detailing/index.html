About Us

Precision care, delivered to your door

Revived Rides Mobile Detailing is an Atlanta-based detailing studio that brings professional-grade results directly to you. We specialize in full interior and exterior detailing, ceramic coatings, and paint correction — without you ever leaving home.

As a locally owned and operated business, we take pride in knowing our city and our clients. Every vehicle is treated with the same care and precision we'd give our own. Our fully equipped mobile setup means no water or power supply needed on your end — just show us to your car.

FAQ

Common questions answered

What areas do you serve?

We offer mobile detailing services at your location, covering a wide service area, typically covering the I-285 area. Please contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.

How long does a full detail take?

A full interior and exterior detail typically takes 2 to 4 hours depending on the vehicle size and condition. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

Do you use eco-friendly products?

Yes, we use high-quality and environmentally responsible products to clean and protect your vehicle safely. We care about the planet as much as your paint.

Do I need to provide water and/or electricity?

No — we are fully equipped with our own water and power supply facilities. Just show us to your vehicle and we handle the rest.

Do I need to be present during the detail?

Nope! As long as we have access to the vehicle, you don't need to be there. You're free to go about your day while we transform your car.

Why should I choose Revived Rides?

We provide professional-level results, convenient mobile service, and treat every car like our own. As a locally owned Atlanta business with a 5.0 rating, we're committed to earning your trust on every visit.
