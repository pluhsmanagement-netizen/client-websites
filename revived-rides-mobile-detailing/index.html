/ 05 — FAQ

Common Questions

Things people ask.

Don't see your question? Reach out and we'll answer in detail.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Atlanta and the inside-I-285 area, including Buckhead, Midtown, Decatur, Sandy Springs, Brookhaven, Dunwoody, and surrounding neighborhoods. If you're nearby and not sure, just ask.

How long does a full detail take?

A full interior and exterior detail typically takes 2 to 4 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. Ceramic coatings and paint correction can take a full day. We'll give you an honest time estimate when you book.

Do I need to provide water or electricity?

No — we're fully self-contained with our own water tank and generator. All we need is access to your vehicle. Show us the car, and we handle the rest.

Do I need to be home during the detail?

Nope. As long as we have access to the vehicle, you're free to go about your day. Many clients leave for work or errands and come back to a transformed car.

Do you use eco-friendly products?

Yes — we use professional-grade products that are safe for your vehicle, your driveway, and the environment. We care about the planet as much as your paint.

What makes Revived Rides different?

We're owner-operated. Every detail is performed by the founders — no subcontractors, no rotating crews. Combine that with our 5.0 rating, fully equipped mobile rig, and genuine love for cars, and you get a level of care most shops can't match.
