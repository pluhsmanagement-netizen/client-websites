FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a roof replacement take in Atlanta, GA?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta can be completed in a single day. Depending on the size and complexity of your roof, some projects may take 2 days. Our crews are efficient and work hard to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
What kind of warranty do you offer on roof replacements?
We offer 50-year warranty coverage on our roof replacements — that's a lifetime of protection for your investment. This industry-leading warranty reflects our commitment to quality materials and superior craftsmanship on every single job.
Do you help with storm damage insurance claims in Atlanta?
Absolutely. Our team has extensive experience working directly with insurance adjusters on storm damage claims. We can meet with your adjuster on your behalf and guide you through the entire claims process to ensure you get the coverage you deserve.
Are you licensed and insured to work in Georgia?
Yes. Roofing Done Wright is fully licensed, insured, and bonded to perform roofing work in the state of Georgia. You can have complete peace of mind knowing your home and our workers are fully protected throughout your project.
Do you offer free roofing estimates in Atlanta?
Yes, we offer completely free roofing estimates to homeowners, realtors, and property managers throughout the Atlanta metro area. Simply call us at (678) 722-5050 or fill out our contact form and we'll schedule your inspection at a convenient time.
What areas near Atlanta do you serve?
We proudly serve Atlanta and the surrounding metro area including Marietta, Smyrna, Sandy Springs, Roswell, Alpharetta, Decatur, and many other communities throughout the greater Atlanta region. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.
