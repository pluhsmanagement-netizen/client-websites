FAQ

Questions
Answered

Everything you need to know before starting your roofing project in Atlanta.

Call Us Directly

How long does a full roof replacement take in Atlanta?
Most residential roof replacements in Atlanta are completed in a single day, typically 5–8 hours depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We keep you updated with progress photos throughout the day so you always know how the project is advancing.

Does Roofing Done Wright help with insurance claims?
Yes — we work directly with insurance adjusters on your behalf and guide you through the entire claims process. Our team documents all storm or hail damage thoroughly so your claim is as strong as possible, and we handle the complexity so you don't have to.

What does the 50-year warranty cover?
Our 50-year warranty covers both materials and workmanship on qualifying roof replacements installed with Owens Corning Total Protection Roofing System. As a Platinum Preferred Contractor, we can offer warranty protection that most roofers simply can't match — it's the strongest coverage in the industry.

How much does a new roof cost in Atlanta?
Roof replacement costs in Atlanta vary based on roof size, pitch, materials selected, and any underlying repairs needed. We provide free, detailed estimates with no pressure — and we'll clearly explain every line item so you understand exactly what you're paying for before any work begins.

Do you serve areas outside Atlanta proper?
Absolutely. We serve the greater Atlanta metro area including Marietta, Alpharetta, Decatur, Kennesaw, Smyrna, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Roswell, and surrounding communities. Call us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

What shingle styles and colors do you offer?
As an Owens Corning Platinum Preferred Contractor, we offer the full range of Owens Corning shingle styles and colors — from architectural to designer profiles. We also provide digital visualization tools so you can preview how different styles will look on your actual home before you commit.
