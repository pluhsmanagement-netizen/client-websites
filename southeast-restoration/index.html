Frequently Asked

Questions answered.

What areas do you serve?

Southeast Restoration serves Canton, Woodstock, Ball Ground, Holly Springs, Jasper, Alpharetta, Cumming, Dahlonega, Dawsonville, Cartersville, and the surrounding Cherokee County area. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, give us a call at (770) 345-3500 and we'll confirm immediately.

Are you available for emergency restoration services?

Yes — Southeast Restoration operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Whether it's a burst pipe at 2am or storm damage on a holiday weekend, we respond fast and get to work. Emergency response is a cornerstone of how we serve our Canton neighbors.

Do you work directly with insurance companies?

Absolutely. Our team has extensive experience navigating insurance claims and will advocate on your behalf throughout the process. We work to ensure your claim covers the full scope of damage and coordinate directly with adjusters so you don't have to manage the paperwork alone.

How long does a typical restoration project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope — mitigation work can begin same-day and be completed within days, while full reconstruction projects may span several weeks. Your dedicated project manager will give you a realistic timeline at the outset and communicate proactively if anything changes.

Is Southeast Restoration licensed and insured?

Yes. Southeast Restoration is fully licensed and insured to operate in Georgia. We carry all required coverages to protect you, your property, and our team throughout every phase of a project. We're happy to provide documentation upon request before work begins.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Getting started is simple. You can call us directly at (770) 345-3500, fill out the contact form on this page, or stop by our Canton office at 120 Mountain Brook Dr. We offer free estimates and will walk through the project scope with you before any work begins — no pressure, no obligation.
