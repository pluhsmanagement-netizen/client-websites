Top Tier Trees is Marietta's premier tree care company — locally based, family-owned, and operated by ISA Certified Arborists who care deeply about the health of your trees and the safety of your property. Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates for tree removal in Marietta, GA?
Yes! You can call us with an emergency or a minor concern for a free quote — no obligation required. A live person answers your call and we'll schedule a certified arborist to visit your property at a time that works for you.
Are you licensed and insured for tree service in Georgia?
Absolutely. Top Tier Trees is fully licensed and insured, with ISA Certified Arborists on staff. Our skilled arbor representatives are trained in the latest safety techniques so you can trust we'll protect your property and our crew throughout every project.
Do you provide 24/7 emergency tree removal services?
Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency tree removal for storm-damaged or fallen trees that pose an immediate danger. We mobilize quickly with the crew and heavy equipment needed to make your property safe, and we're happy to speak with your insurance adjuster on your behalf.
Do you handle commercial tree care for businesses in Marietta?
Yes — we specialize in commercial tree care and offer proactive annual tree care planning for commercial properties to keep your business safe year-round. From trimming and pruning to full removal and tree inventories, we handle everything so you don't need multiple contractors.
What areas do you serve beyond Marietta, GA?
Top Tier Trees serves Marietta, East Cobb, Kennesaw, Roswell, Woodstock, Sandy Springs, Smyrna, Acworth, Canton, and the surrounding areas of metro Atlanta. If you're not sure we cover your location, just give us a call — we're happy to help.
How does the tree removal process work from start to finish?
It starts with a free estimate from one of our ISA Certified Arborists who evaluates your trees and provides a competitive quote. We handle any required city permits, schedule the work at your convenience, and our crew arrives on time with all necessary equipment. After the job, we perform a thorough cleanup leaving your property in better condition than we found it.
